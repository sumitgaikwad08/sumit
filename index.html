Colors and Their Meanings

Red

Red is often associated with passion, energy, and action. It can also represent danger or warning.

Blue

Blue is typically associated with calmness, trust, and professionalism. It's often used in corporate settings.

Green

Green represents growth, nature, and tranquility. It's often linked to sustainability and environmental awareness.

Yellow

Yellow is associated with happiness, optimism, and creativity. It can also be a warning color in certain contexts.

Black

Black often represents elegance, mystery, and sophistication. It can also symbolize authority or mourning.

White

White is linked to purity, simplicity, and peace. It is often associated with cleanliness and minimalism.
